Exercises

Exercise 1: PIR Motion with Status LEDs

PIR • LEDs • LCD

PIR triggers RED LED and "Detected!" message on LCD. No motion shows GREEN LED and "Ready". 30s warmup blinks ORANGE LED.

Input: PIR sensor (D22) Output: Red (D5), Green (D6), Orange (D13), LCD
Ready

Exercise 2: Gas Detection

Gas Sensor • LED • LCD

Gas level monitoring with safe/alert status on LCD and LED indicator.

Input: MQ Gas sensor Output: Red/Green LED + LCD
Ready

Exercise 3: Temperature & Humidity

DHT11 • LCD

Shows real-time temperature (°C) and humidity (%) on LCD via I²C.

Input: DHT11 Output: LCD
Ready

Exercise 4: Distance Measurement

Ultrasonic • Buzzer • LCD

Displays distance in cm on LCD. Buzzer beeps when object is near.

Input: HC-SR04 Ultrasonic Output: LCD + Buzzer
Ready

Exercise 5: Air Pressure Reading

BMP280 • I²C • LCD

Shows temperature (°C) and pressure (hPa/kPa) from BMP280 on LCD.

Input: BMP280 (mux CH2) Output: LCD
Ready
